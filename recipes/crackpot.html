Viking Crackpot Stew

Prep time: 10 minutes. Cook time: 5 hours. Makes 4 servings.

    Ingredients
  • 4 large onions - roughly diced
  • 8 cloves garlic - crushed
  • 4 large potatoes - chopped into chunks
  • 900 g (2 lb) Meat of choice - Beef shin, Venison, Smoked sausage
  • 450 g (1 lb) bacon - optional but necessary and chopped roughly
  • 1 cabbage - or other vegetables like carrot or celeriac roughly chopped or grated
  • 1000 ml (4 ⅕ cups) beer - Dark beer works best
  • 1000 ml (4 ⅕ cups) water/meat or vegetable stock
    Directions
  1. Place all the ingredients together into the pot and mix it well.
  2. Cook it at low medium temperature for about 5 hours monitoring it every now and again adding water or stock if needed.
  3. Season with salt and pepper when done.
  4. You can also chop up some herbs(parsley, marjoram, dill)and add it in when done.
